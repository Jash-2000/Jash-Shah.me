Home

Hi ! I am Jash Shah, a pre-final year Electronics and Instrumentation engineering student from Birla Institute of Technology and Science ( BITS P), Pilani campus and am expecting to complete my Undergraduate studies ( B.E (Hons.) ) in May 2022.

 I am interested in technologies that corresponds to integrating Hardware with Software i.e. Embedded Systems and related Sensor arrays. This was the very reason I opted for Instrumentation Engineering. I have always been interested in engaging into multi-disciplinary projects and would love to explore as many fields as possible. In general, I love experimenting and innovating stuffs. Over the span of 2 years of my college life, other than Electronics and related fields, I have also explored Embedded Systems and IoT, OS and networking, Robotics and Automated Control System, AI – ML and algorithms. I have even tried my hands on Blockchain and ethical hacking, and would love explore recent fields like swarm agent theory, Medical Instrumentation and Cognitive Neuroscience and Haptic VR/AR.

I love bird watching, and listening to Hindi Bollywood music. I am a professional trekker and have completed many treks during my summer vacations. To, add on, I also hold a certified degree in painting, and have also passed the Level 2 Indian Classical Music Examination for both vocal (singing) and instrumental ( keyboard ). I have also been into the district Chess team.

The Malaysian book of Records also holds my name for being in the batch that passed the ORDINARY Grade 11 International Standard of Abacus Computation, in 2009.

I am hardcore patriot and it’s my goal to open a high-end Research and Development center in India.
